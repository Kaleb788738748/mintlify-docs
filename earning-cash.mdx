Every action you take in Match & Cash can add to your real-money balance. Your balance is displayed at the top-left of the screen and accumulates from multiple sources: clearing levels, completing Daily Missions, watching bonus ads, and participating in limited-time events. When you're ready, tap **Cash Out** to convert your balance to PayPal, Cash App, or a gift card.

## Ways to Earn

### 1. Clear Levels

Every time you complete a level by filling the progress bar to 100%, a cash reward is added to your balance. Rewards increase as you progress to higher, more challenging levels.

### 2. Daily Missions

The **Daily Missions** panel appears at the bottom of the game screen. Each day a new mission is set — for example:

> **Play 120 minutes** → Earn **$5**

The mission shows a progress bar (e.g., 97/120) so you can see exactly how close you are to the reward. Once you hit the target, the cash is credited to your balance automatically.

<Info>
Daily Missions reset at midnight. Complete each day's mission before it resets to maximize your earnings.
</Info>

### 3. Watch Ads for Bonus Coins

Tap the ad banner (🎬 icon) in the lower-right area of the game screen to watch a short video ad. Each completed ad awards **+30 coins**. Use those coins to buy props that help you clear harder levels faster — keeping your earning momentum going.

### 4. Claim Free Props

Some props can be **claimed for free** by watching a short ad instead of spending coins. When a Prop popup appears, tap **▶ Claim** to watch an ad and receive the prop at no coin cost. Free props help you maintain your streak without depleting your coin supply.

### 5. In-Game Money Bundles

Money bundle items (the green $ icons on the shelves) count as matchable goods. Collecting three money bundle items clears them like any other match and adds a direct bonus to your cash balance.

## Your Balance

Your current balance is always visible in the top-left corner of the screen. It updates in real time as you earn. There is no limit on how high your balance can grow — keep playing to keep earning.
