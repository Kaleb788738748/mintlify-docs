Match & Cash is easy to learn but rewarding to master. Each level gives you a set of shelves packed with goods — your goal is to clear the shelves by matching identical items three at a time. This guide walks you through everything you need to know to play confidently from your first level.

## The Game Screen

When you open a level you'll see:

- **Shelves** — stacked rows of goods filling most of the screen.
- **Tray** — the row of slots at the bottom where selected goods land.
- **Balance** — your real-money total shown in the top-left corner (e.g., $471.04).
- **Coins** — your coin count shown next to the balance.
- **Progress bar** — the orange bar on the right edge showing level completion percentage.
- **Props** — three icons at the very bottom: Recall One Good, Remove 3 Goods, and Rearrange All Goods.

## Core Gameplay Loop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap a good to select it">
    Tap any visible item on the shelves. It moves down to your tray at the bottom. You can hold up to seven items in the tray at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect three matching items">
    Tap two more goods of the same type. When the tray contains three identical items, they automatically clear and your cash balance increases.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Keep the tray from filling">
    If all seven tray slots are occupied before you complete a match, the level ends. Plan your taps so you don't get stuck with mismatched items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the level">
    Clear enough goods to reach 100% on the progress bar. Completing the level grants a bonus cash reward and unlocks the next level.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tray Management Tips

The tray is the most critical resource in the game. Keep these rules in mind:

<Note>
You have **7 tray slots**. Never let them fill with unmatched items — always plan at least one clear pair before tapping a new type of good.
</Note>

- **Group by type** — try to collect two of the same item before tapping a different type.
- **Prioritize accessible goods** — goods in the lower shelves are tapped first; clearing them reveals items hidden above.
- **Watch the stack** — some shelves have stacked items. Tap the bottom item to expose the one on top.

## Level Completion & Progress

The orange progress bar on the right edge of the screen shows how close you are to finishing the level. The bar fills as you clear matched sets. Most levels end at 100%, but some special levels have a different target shown at the top of the bar.

When you complete a level:
- A cash reward is added to your balance.
- A "Level Complete" animation plays.
- The next level unlocks automatically.

<Tip>
Use props when the shelves get tight — don't save them for a perfect moment. A well-timed Rearrange or Remove prop can save a level that looks impossible.
</Tip>
