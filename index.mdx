Sort fashion and beauty goods on shelves, match three identical items, rack up your in-game balance, and cash out to the payment method of your choice — including PayPal, Cash App, Amazon gift cards, Walmart, DoorDash, Target, and LEGO gift cards.

## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Install Match & Cash from the App Store or Google Play. Open the app and create your free account — no credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play your first level">
    Tap goods on the shelves to send matching items to the bottom tray. Collect three of the same item to clear them and earn in-game cash.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Daily Missions">
    Check the **Daily Missions** panel each day for bonus cash rewards. Playing 120 minutes, for example, earns an extra $5 added directly to your balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cash out your balance">
    Once you reach the payout threshold, tap **Cash Out** and choose your preferred method: Cash App, PayPal, Amazon, Walmart, DoorDash, Target, or LEGO gift cards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Watch short ads to earn +30 coins instantly. Use those coins to buy props that help you clear difficult shelves and keep your balance growing faster.
</Tip>
