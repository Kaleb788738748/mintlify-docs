Match & Cash is a mobile puzzle game where you sort and organize fashion and beauty goods on crowded shelves — and get paid real money for doing it. The more you match, the higher your in-game balance grows, and when you're ready you can convert that balance into cash via PayPal or Cash App, or redeem it as a gift card at Amazon, Walmart, DoorDash, Target, or LEGO.

## What is Match & Cash?

In each level, you see a set of shelves stacked with colorful goods: perfume bottles, handbags, beauty tubes, accessory items, and money bundles. Your job is to tap goods to move them to a holding tray at the bottom of the screen. When you collect **three identical items** in the tray, they automatically clear and your cash balance increases.

The game tracks two currencies:

| Currency | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Cash ($)** | Your real-money balance, displayed in the top-left corner. Cash out when you hit the threshold. |
| **Coins** | In-game currency used to purchase power-up props. Earn coins by watching ads. |

## Who is Match & Cash for?

Match & Cash is for anyone who enjoys casual mobile puzzles and wants to earn meaningful rewards while playing. No special skills are required — just an eye for matching and a bit of strategy when the shelves get crowded.
