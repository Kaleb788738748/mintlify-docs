Daily Missions are time-limited challenges that reward you with real cash simply for playing Match & Cash consistently. Unlike regular level rewards, Daily Missions set a specific goal — such as playing for a total of 120 minutes — and offer a larger, fixed cash payout when you reach it. They're one of the best ways to earn meaningful rewards in the game, and checking them every day should be a core part of your Match & Cash routine.

## What Daily Missions Are

Each day, you receive one or more missions with a clear objective and a defined cash prize attached to it. The mission counter tracks your progress automatically as you play — you don't need to do anything special to start earning progress, just open the game and play levels as normal.

<Info>
  Daily Missions reward sustained play rather than pure skill. Even if you're still getting comfortable with the matching mechanics, you can fully complete missions just by putting in the time.
</Info>

### Example Mission

A typical mission looks like this:

> **Play 120 minutes → Earn $5**
> Progress bar: 97 / 120 minutes

The progress bar fills as you accumulate playtime throughout the day. In this example, you've already logged 97 minutes and only need 23 more to claim the $5 reward.

## How to Access Daily Missions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Daily Missions panel">
    Tap the **Daily Missions** icon at the bottom of the game screen. It's visible from both the main menu and the level-select screen, so you can check your progress without starting a level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your active missions">
    The panel shows each active mission, the target goal, and a progress bar indicating how close you are to completion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play levels to advance your progress">
    Return to playing levels normally. Your playtime and any other mission criteria are tracked automatically in the background.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim your reward when complete">
    When the progress bar reaches 100%, the mission is marked complete and the cash reward is added to your balance. You may need to tap a **Claim** button to officially collect it — check the missions panel after you cross the threshold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tracking Your Progress

The progress bar inside each mission updates in real time as you play. For a playtime mission, every minute you spend in an active level counts toward your total.

<Note>
  Progress is only counted while you are actively playing. Time spent on the main menu, in settings, or with the app open but in the background does not advance your mission timer.
</Note>

The bar shows both the numeric progress (e.g., **97/120**) and a visual fill so you can gauge at a glance how close you are without doing the math.

## Reset Schedule

Daily Missions reset every day at **midnight local time**. When the clock ticks over to a new day, your current missions expire and a fresh set of missions becomes available.

<Warning>
  Any incomplete mission progress is lost at the daily reset. If you're at 97/120 minutes and don't finish before midnight, that progress does not carry over to the next day. Plan your sessions so you have enough time to reach the goal before the reset.
</Warning>

## How Rewards Are Credited

When you complete a Daily Mission, the cash reward is added directly to your in-game cash balance. From there, you can redeem your accumulated earnings through the game's payout options, including **Cash App, PayPal, Amazon, Walmart, DoorDash, Target, and LEGO** gift cards.

<Info>
  Daily Mission rewards count toward the same cash balance as your level-completion earnings. There's no separate wallet — everything pools together for redemption.
</Info>

## Tips for Completing Daily Missions

<Accordion title="Check missions first thing every day">
  Open the Daily Missions panel as soon as you launch the game each day. Knowing your goal upfront lets you plan your session length. If the mission requires 120 minutes of play, you know to spread your sessions across the day rather than trying to squeeze it all in at the last minute.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Plan sessions around the mission target">
  Break the required playtime into chunks that fit your schedule. For a 120-minute mission, three 40-minute sessions spread across morning, afternoon, and evening is far more comfortable than one marathon sitting. Set a reminder on your phone if needed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Play before midnight, not after">
  If you're close to completing a mission late in the evening, prioritize finishing it before the midnight reset rather than stopping and hoping you'll have time the next morning. The next day's missions might have different requirements entirely.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Stack missions with coin-earning">
  Extended play sessions required by Daily Missions are also a great opportunity to watch ads between levels and stock up on coins. You're already playing — make those minutes work double duty by building your coin reserve while progressing toward your mission goal. See the [Coins guide](/mechanics/coins) for details.
</Accordion>

<Tip>
  Completing Daily Missions consistently is one of the highest-value activities in Match & Cash. Even on days when you're not aiming for a high level count, simply logging the required playtime can put a meaningful cash reward in your balance with minimal effort.
</Tip>
