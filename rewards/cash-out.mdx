Match & Cash lets you turn every shelf you sort into real money. Once your balance builds up — from matching goods, completing daily missions, and finishing levels — you can convert it into actual cash or gift cards whenever you're ready. The **Cash Out** button sits right next to your balance in the top header, so your earnings are always one tap away.

## What Is the Cash Out Feature?

The Cash Out feature is your gateway to converting your in-game balance into real-world value. Whether you prefer instant cash through Cash App, a PayPal transfer, or a gift card for your favourite retailer, Match & Cash gives you flexibility in how you get paid. Your balance — displayed prominently at the top of the screen (for example, **$471.04**) — reflects everything you've earned and is always ready to be redeemed.

## How to Access Cash Out

You don't need to dig through menus to find your earnings. The **Cash Out** button is permanently visible in the top header of the game, right alongside your current balance. Tap it at any time to begin the withdrawal process.

## Step-by-Step Cash Out Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap the Cash Out Button">
    From anywhere in the game, look at the top of the screen. You'll see your current balance (e.g., **$471.04**) and a **Cash Out** button right next to it. Tap that button to open the payout screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Payout Method">
    You'll be presented with all available payout options:
    - **Cash App** — receive funds to your \$Cashtag
    - **PayPal** — transfer to your PayPal account
    - **Gift Cards** — Amazon, Walmart, DoorDash, Target, or LEGO

    Select the method that works best for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Account Details">
    Depending on the method you chose, you'll be prompted to enter the relevant details:
    - For **Cash App**: your \$Cashtag
    - For **PayPal**: the email address linked to your PayPal account
    - For **Gift Cards**: the email address where you'd like the code delivered
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Your Withdrawal">
    Review the payout amount and your account details carefully. Once everything looks correct, tap **Confirm** to submit your cash-out request. You'll receive an in-app confirmation once it's been processed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Double-check your \$Cashtag, PayPal email, or gift card delivery email before confirming. Payments sent to incorrect account details cannot be reversed, so accuracy here is essential.
</Note>

## Minimum Payout Thresholds

Like most mobile reward apps, Match & Cash requires a minimum balance before you can cash out. Thresholds are typically in the **$5–$10 range**, though the exact amount may vary by payout method or as part of limited-time promotions. Always check the app for the current minimum thresholds before attempting a withdrawal.

<Info>
  If the Cash Out button appears greyed out or disabled, it likely means your balance hasn't yet reached the minimum threshold for your selected payout method. Keep playing to build your balance!
</Info>

## Processing Times

Not every payout method delivers at the same speed. Here's what to generally expect:

| Payout Method | Typical Processing Time |
|---|---|
| Cash App | Near-instant (usually within minutes) |
| PayPal | 1–3 business days |
| Amazon Gift Card | 1–3 business days (email delivery) |
| Walmart Gift Card | 1–3 business days (email delivery) |
| DoorDash Gift Card | 1–3 business days (email delivery) |
| Target Gift Card | 1–3 business days (email delivery) |
| LEGO Gift Card | 1–3 business days (email delivery) |

<Info>
  Processing times are estimates and may occasionally be longer due to high request volumes or verification requirements. If your payout hasn't arrived after 5 business days, contact Match & Cash support.
</Info>

## Tips for a Smooth Cash-Out Experience

<Tip>
  **Save your account details in the app.** If the app allows you to save your \$Cashtag or PayPal email, do it early — it reduces errors and makes future cash-outs even faster.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Cash out regularly.** Don't let a huge balance sit idle. Smaller, more frequent cash-outs reduce the risk of any issues and get your rewards into your hands sooner.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Choose the right method for your needs.** If you want immediate access to funds, Cash App is your best bet. If you shop frequently at a particular retailer, a matching gift card may stretch your rewards further than cash.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Make sure you are using the latest version of the Match & Cash app before attempting to cash out. Outdated versions may experience errors during the payout process.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I enter the wrong payment details?">
    Payouts sent to incorrect account details (wrong \$Cashtag, PayPal email, or delivery email) generally cannot be reversed. Always verify your details on the confirmation screen before tapping **Confirm**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel a cash-out after confirming?">
    Once a payout request is confirmed, it typically cannot be cancelled. If you believe there's been an error, contact Match & Cash support as quickly as possible — there may be a brief window in which they can assist.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is my Cash Out button greyed out?">
    The most common reason is that your balance is below the minimum payout threshold. It may also appear inactive if there's a temporary hold on your account. Check the app for any notifications or messages about your account status.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I split a payout across multiple methods?">
    Currently, each cash-out request is directed to a single payout method. If you'd like to use multiple methods, you can initiate separate cash-out requests (subject to minimum thresholds each time).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
