Gift cards are one of the most popular ways to redeem your Match & Cash balance, and for good reason — they let you put your earnings to work at the retailers and services you already use every day. Whether you're stocking up on Amazon essentials, grabbing a Walmart haul, ordering dinner through DoorDash, shopping Target's latest deals, or building a new LEGO set, your in-game rewards can go further than you might expect.

## Overview of Gift Card Redemption

When you choose a gift card as your payout method in Match & Cash, you'll receive a unique redemption code delivered to your email address. This code represents the value of your payout and can be applied at checkout — either online or, in many cases, in-store — at the relevant retailer. Gift cards are a great option if you already shop at a supported retailer frequently, since you're essentially getting paid to play in the form of store credit.

<Info>
  Gift card delivery typically takes **1–3 business days** after your cash-out request is confirmed. Check your inbox (and spam/junk folder) for an email containing your code.
</Info>

## How Gift Cards Are Delivered

After your cash-out request is processed, your gift card code is sent to the email address you provided during the checkout flow. The email will include:

- The **gift card brand** and **value**
- Your unique **redemption code**
- A link or instructions for **where and how to redeem** the code

<Note>
  Always enter the email address you actively check. If you haven't received your code within 3 business days, check your spam or junk mail folder before contacting support.
</Note>

## How to Redeem Each Gift Card

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Amazon Gift Card">
    **Redeem at:** [Amazon.com](https://www.amazon.com)

    1. Sign in to your Amazon account at **Amazon.com**.
    2. Navigate to **Account & Lists → Gift cards → Redeem a Gift Card**.
    3. Enter your claim code in the field provided and click **Apply to your balance**.
    4. Your Amazon balance will be updated immediately and applied automatically at checkout on your next order.

    Amazon gift cards never expire and can be used on any eligible product sold by Amazon or third-party sellers on the platform.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Walmart Gift Card">
    **Redeem at:** Walmart stores or [Walmart.com](https://www.walmart.com)

    **Online:**
    1. Add items to your cart at **Walmart.com** and proceed to checkout.
    2. In the payment section, select **Gift Card** and enter your code.
    3. The card value will be applied to your order total.

    **In-Store:**
    - Present your gift card code at the register or customer service desk. Some locations allow associates to manually apply the code; check with your local store for details.

    Walmart gift cards are valid on groceries, electronics, clothing, household items, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="DoorDash Gift Card">
    **Redeem at:** DoorDash app or [DoorDash.com](https://www.doordash.com)

    1. Open the **DoorDash app** or visit **DoorDash.com** and sign in.
    2. Tap your profile icon and go to **Credits & Gift Cards**.
    3. Select **Redeem Gift Card** and enter your code.
    4. The credit will be added to your DoorDash account and applied automatically at checkout on your next order.

    DoorDash gift cards can be used for food delivery orders and, where available, to purchase a **DashPass** subscription for unlimited free delivery.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Target Gift Card">
    **Redeem at:** Target stores or [Target.com](https://www.target.com)

    **Online:**
    1. Shop at **Target.com** and proceed to checkout.
    2. In the payment section, select **Gift Card** and enter your card number and PIN (if applicable).
    3. The balance will be applied to your order.

    **In-Store:**
    - At the register, inform the cashier you're paying with a gift card and provide the code. Target store associates can manually apply the code.

    Target gift cards work across all departments, including grocery (at Target stores with a full grocery section), apparel, home, electronics, and more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="LEGO Gift Card">
    **Redeem at:** [LEGO.com](https://www.lego.com) or official LEGO Stores

    **Online:**
    1. Shop at **LEGO.com** and proceed to checkout.
    2. In the payment section, select **Gift Card** and enter your code.
    3. The value will be deducted from your order total.

    **In-Store:**
    - Visit an official LEGO Store and present your gift card code at the register. Store associates will apply the balance to your purchase.

    LEGO gift cards are perfect for purchasing sets, minifigures, accessories, and exclusive products available only through LEGO's official channels.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Tips for Choosing the Right Gift Card

<Tip>
  **Match the card to your regular spending.** If you order groceries from Walmart weekly or shop Amazon for household items, a gift card for that retailer effectively doubles the value of your rewards by replacing spending you'd do anyway.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **DoorDash is great for a treat.** If you rarely indulge in food delivery, a DoorDash gift card is a perfect excuse to enjoy a meal without spending your own money.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **LEGO cards make excellent gifts.** If you're not a LEGO shopper yourself but know someone who is, a LEGO gift card is a thoughtful present that costs you only in-game effort.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Gift card codes are typically single-use and non-refundable. Store them safely and redeem them promptly. Match & Cash cannot replace lost or stolen codes once they've been delivered to your email.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do gift cards have an expiration date?">
    Expiration policies vary by retailer. Amazon gift cards, for example, do not expire. Always review the terms for your specific gift card brand after receiving your code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a gift card code partially?">
    Yes — most retailers (including Amazon, Walmart, and Target) allow partial redemption. Any remaining balance stays on the card or your account for future use.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my gift card code doesn't work?">
    First, confirm you've entered the code exactly as it appears in your email (check for typos or missed characters). If the code still doesn't work, contact Match & Cash support with your order details and a screenshot of the error.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I convert a gift card back to cash?">
    Gift card values cannot be converted back to cash through Match & Cash. Once a gift card code is issued, it is only redeemable at the designated retailer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
