Match & Cash gives you seven distinct ways to convert your in-game balance into real-world value. From instant cash transfers to gift cards at your favourite retailers, there's a payout option built for every kind of player. Understanding the differences between each method — delivery speed, best use cases, and any requirements — helps you get the most out of every dollar you earn.

## Quick Comparison

Use the table below to get a high-level overview of all available payout methods before diving into the details.

| Method | Type | Delivery | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cash App | Cash transfer | Near-instant | Immediate access to funds |
| PayPal | Cash transfer | 1–3 business days | Broad acceptance, online payments |
| Amazon Gift Card | Gift card (email) | 1–3 business days | Online shopping & everyday essentials |
| Walmart Gift Card | Gift card (email) | 1–3 business days | Groceries, household goods, in-store & online |
| DoorDash Gift Card | Gift card (email) | 1–3 business days | Food delivery & meal treats |
| Target Gift Card | Gift card (email) | 1–3 business days | Style, home, grocery, and more |
| LEGO Gift Card | Gift card (email) | 1–3 business days | LEGO sets, accessories, and exclusive products |

<Note>
  Before you initiate any cash-out, have your account details ready — your \$Cashtag, PayPal email address, or gift card delivery email — to avoid delays or errors during the process.
</Note>

## Cash App

Cash App is the fastest way to get your Match & Cash rewards into spendable funds. When you select Cash App as your payout method, the transfer is sent directly to your Cash App account using your **\$Cashtag** — the unique username associated with your Cash App profile.

**How it works:**
- Enter your **\$Cashtag** (e.g., `$YourName`) during the cash-out process.
- Funds are typically deposited near-instantly, often within minutes of your request being approved.
- Once received, you can spend your Cash App balance using your Cash App Card, send it to your bank account, or use it for peer-to-peer payments.

<Tip>
  Cash App is the ideal choice if you want your money fast. It's perfect for players who want immediate access without waiting for bank processing windows.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Your \$Cashtag is case-sensitive and must be entered exactly as it appears in your Cash App profile. Sending funds to the wrong \$Cashtag is irreversible.
</Warning>

## PayPal

PayPal is one of the world's most trusted digital payment platforms, accepted at millions of online retailers and usable for peer-to-peer transfers. Match & Cash sends your payout to the **email address linked to your PayPal account**.

**How it works:**
- Enter the **email address** associated with your PayPal account during the cash-out process.
- Funds typically arrive within **1–3 business days** after your request is approved.
- Once in your PayPal balance, you can spend it online, transfer it to your bank account, or send it to someone else.

<Tip>
  PayPal is the best choice if you regularly shop at online retailers that accept PayPal, or if you want a digital wallet that bridges easily to your bank account.
</Tip>

<Info>
  If you don't yet have a PayPal account, you can create one for free at [paypal.com](https://www.paypal.com). Make sure to verify your account to avoid receiving limits.
</Info>

## Amazon Gift Card

An Amazon gift card turns your Match & Cash earnings into purchasing power on one of the world's largest online marketplaces. Your gift card code is delivered to your email and can be applied to any eligible Amazon.com purchase.

**How it works:**
- Enter your **email address** during the cash-out process — your code will be delivered there.
- Codes arrive within **1–3 business days**.
- Redeem your code by going to **Account & Lists → Gift Cards → Redeem a Gift Card** on Amazon.com.
- Your Amazon balance is credited instantly upon redemption and applied automatically at checkout.

<Tip>
  Amazon gift cards never expire, making them a safe choice if you're not in a rush to spend. They're ideal for regular Amazon shoppers or anyone planning a larger purchase.
</Tip>

## Walmart Gift Card

Walmart gift cards give you access to one of America's most versatile retailers — in-store and online. From groceries and electronics to clothing and home goods, your rewards can stretch across a huge range of everyday needs.

**How it works:**
- Enter your **email address** during the cash-out process.
- Your gift card code is delivered to your inbox within **1–3 business days**.
- Use the code at **Walmart.com** checkout (under the Gift Card payment option) or present it at any **Walmart store** register.

<Tip>
  Walmart gift cards are one of the most practical options if you do regular grocery runs or household shopping. Applying a gift card to purchases you'd make anyway effectively makes your gameplay free money.
</Tip>

## DoorDash Gift Card

DoorDash gift cards are a great way to reward yourself with a meal — paid for entirely by your in-game earnings. Apply your code in the DoorDash app and use it toward your next delivery order or DashPass subscription.

**How it works:**
- Enter your **email address** during the cash-out process.
- Your code arrives within **1–3 business days**.
- Open the **DoorDash app**, go to **Profile → Credits & Gift Cards → Redeem Gift Card**, and enter your code.
- The credit is added to your account and automatically applied at checkout.

<Tip>
  DoorDash gift cards are perfect for players who want to treat themselves without dipping into their own wallet. They're especially valuable if you use DashPass, as credits apply toward subscription costs too.
</Tip>

---

## Target Gift Card

Target gift cards work across Target's full range of departments — apparel, home décor, electronics, beauty, grocery, and more. Redeem your code online at Target.com or in any Target store.

**How it works:**
- Enter your **email address** during the cash-out process.
- Your code arrives within **1–3 business days**.
- **Online:** Apply your code in the Gift Card field at Target.com checkout.
- **In-store:** Present your code at the register; a store associate will apply it to your purchase.

<Tip>
  Target's wide product range makes this gift card one of the most flexible options for shoppers who like variety. It's a strong choice for parents, home decorators, and anyone who makes regular Target runs.
</Tip>

## LEGO Gift Card

LEGO gift cards are a specialty option perfect for fans of the iconic brick brand. Redeem your code at LEGO.com or at official LEGO Stores for sets, minifigures, accessories, and exclusive products you won't find anywhere else.

**How it works:**
- Enter your **email address** during the cash-out process.
- Your code arrives within **1–3 business days**.
- **Online:** Apply your code in the Gift Card field at [LEGO.com](https://www.lego.com) checkout.
- **In-store:** Present your code at any official LEGO Store register.

<Tip>
  LEGO gift cards are ideal if you're a collector, a parent buying gifts, or a fan saving up for a large exclusive set. They also make thoughtful presents for LEGO enthusiasts in your life.
</Tip>

## How to Choose the Right Payout Method

With seven options available, picking the right one comes down to a few simple questions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I want my money as fast as possible">
    Choose **Cash App**. It's the only near-instant payout option, with funds typically arriving within minutes of your request being approved. All other methods involve at least a 1–3 business day delay.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want flexible digital cash I can use anywhere">
    Choose **PayPal**. With PayPal accepted at millions of merchants worldwide and easy bank transfer capabilities, it offers the broadest spending flexibility among the non-instant options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I shop online regularly">
    Choose **Amazon** or **Walmart** gift cards. Both cover a massive range of products. Amazon is better for pure online shopping; Walmart is better if you also want in-store flexibility.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want to treat myself to food delivery">
    Choose **DoorDash**. Your gift card credit applies directly to delivery orders, so you can enjoy a restaurant-quality meal without spending a cent of your own money.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want a versatile in-store and online option">
    Choose **Target**. Its wide range of departments — from fashion and home to electronics and groceries — makes it one of the most all-around useful gift card options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm a LEGO fan or buying a gift">
    Choose **LEGO**. LEGO gift cards unlock the full catalog at LEGO.com and official stores, including exclusive sets and limited editions only available through LEGO directly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Before You Cash Out: A Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your balance">
    Make sure your in-game balance meets or exceeds the minimum payout threshold for your chosen method. Check the app for current thresholds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Have your account details ready">
    Locate your \$Cashtag, PayPal email address, or gift card delivery email address before starting the process to avoid errors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your method">
    Use the comparison table and accordion guide above to select the payout method that best fits your needs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your details">
    On the confirmation screen, carefully review the account details you've entered. Incorrect details can result in lost payouts that cannot be recovered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and wait">
    Tap **Confirm** and allow the appropriate processing time. Check your email inbox for gift card codes or your Cash App/PayPal app for cash transfers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If your payout hasn't arrived within 5 business days, contact Match & Cash support with your in-game account details and a description of the issue. Keep any confirmation emails as a reference.
</Note>
