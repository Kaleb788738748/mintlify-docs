Whether you're just getting started or you've been playing for a while, it's natural to have questions about how Match & Cash works — especially when real money is involved. Below you'll find answers to the most frequently asked questions, organized by topic so you can jump straight to what you need.

## Earnings & Payouts

<Accordion title="Is Match & Cash free to play?">
  Yes — Match & Cash is completely free to download and play. You can earn real cash rewards simply by playing levels, completing Daily Missions, and matching goods on the shelves. Optional ads can be watched to earn coins or claim free props, but watching ads is never required to progress. There are no mandatory purchases.

  <Note>
    Watching ads is the primary way to earn coins, which you can then spend on helpful in-game props. It's always your choice whether to watch.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I cash out my earnings?">
  Once your balance meets the minimum payout threshold, you can request a withdrawal directly from the app.

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Open the Rewards or Wallet screen">
      Tap the cash or wallet icon from the main menu to see your current balance.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select 'Cash Out'">
      Choose the Cash Out option and pick your preferred payout method from the available list.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Enter your account details">
      Provide the email address or account information for your chosen payout service (e.g., your PayPal email or Cash App $Cashtag).
    </Step>
    <Step title="Confirm and submit">
      Review the details and confirm your request. You'll receive an in-app confirmation once the request is submitted.
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the minimum amount I need to cash out?">
  The minimum cashout threshold varies by payout method. Check the Cash Out screen within the app for the exact minimum tied to your preferred option, as these amounts can be updated over time.

  <Tip>
    Focus on Daily Missions alongside regular level play — missions often offer the largest single cash bonuses (e.g., $5 for 120 minutes of play) and can help you reach the minimum threshold faster.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my payout?">
  Processing times vary depending on the payout method you choose. Digital options like Cash App and PayPal are typically faster, while gift card codes may take a short time to generate and deliver.

  <Note>
    If your payout hasn't arrived within the expected window shown in the app, check your spam or junk folder (for email-delivered rewards) before contacting support.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do gift card codes get delivered to me?">
  Gift card codes are delivered digitally. Once your payout request is processed, the code is typically sent to the email address associated with your account and/or displayed directly in the app on the rewards screen.

  <Warning>
    Make sure the email address linked to your Match & Cash account is correct and accessible before requesting a gift card payout. Codes sent to an incorrect email address may be difficult to recover.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change my payout method?">
  Yes. You can select a different payout method each time you submit a cash out request — you're not locked into a single option. Simply choose your preferred method on the Cash Out screen at the time of your request.

  <Tip>
    If you're unsure which method to use, Cash App and PayPal tend to have the fastest delivery times for liquid cash, while gift cards are great if you already shop at those retailers regularly.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

## Gameplay

<Accordion title="What happens if my tray fills up?">
  Your tray holds a maximum of 7 goods. If all 7 slots are occupied and none of the items form a matching trio, you'll be unable to tap any more goods from the shelves — the level is effectively jammed.

  At this point, you have two options:

  - **Use a prop** (Recall One Good, Remove 3 Goods, or Rearrange All Goods) to break the jam and free up space.
  - If no props are available and you can't resolve the tray, the level will end without completion.

  <Warning>
    A full tray is the most common reason players fail a level. To avoid it, try to keep at least 2 slots free at all times and always complete a matching pair before introducing new item types. See the [Strategy Guide](/tips/strategy) for full tray management tips.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do Daily Missions work?">
  Daily Missions are bonus objectives that refresh every day and reward you with extra cash for meeting specific play goals. Examples include playing for a set amount of time, completing a certain number of levels, or collecting a target number of matches.

  <Steps>
    <Step title="Open the Daily Missions panel">
      Access it from the main menu at the start of each session to see today's available missions and their rewards.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Play normally">
      Most missions are completed naturally through regular gameplay — no special mode is required.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Claim your reward">
      Once a mission's goal is met, tap 'Claim' to add the cash reward to your balance. Missions do not auto-claim.
    </Step>
  </Steps>

  <Tip>
    Checking missions before you start playing lets you prioritize activities that align with the day's targets — especially if there's a high-value time-based mission available.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do Daily Missions reset every day?">
  Yes. Daily Missions reset at midnight each day. Any uncompleted missions from the previous day are replaced with a fresh set.

  <Warning>
    Completed missions that have **not been claimed** before midnight are lost — their rewards do not carry over to the next day. Always claim your completed missions before the daily reset.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why did my balance not increase after completing a level?">
  There are a few common reasons your balance might not reflect a level completion:

  - **Minimum threshold not yet reached** — some reward increments are only credited once your running total crosses a certain point in the session.
  - **Network delay** — balance updates can occasionally take a moment to sync. Try closing and reopening the app.
  - **Level not fully completed** — if you exited the level before the completion animation finished, the reward may not have been fully registered.
  - **Daily Mission not claimed** — if the cash was tied to a Daily Mission, you need to manually claim it from the missions panel.

  <Note>
    If your balance remains incorrect after trying the above, contact in-app support with a screenshot of your session for assistance.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

## Props & Coins

<Accordion title="What are coins and how do I earn them?">
  Coins are Match & Cash's in-game currency used to purchase props — the special tools that help you when a level gets tough. You cannot cash out coins; they exist solely to power up your gameplay.

  **How to earn coins:**
  - **Watch ads** — each ad you watch earns you **+30 coins**. This is the primary and most reliable way to build your coin balance.
  - **In-game rewards** — certain milestones or events may grant bonus coins.

  <Tip>
    Build a habit of watching one ad between levels. Three ads add up to 90 coins — nearly enough for a full prop — with only a minute or two of your time.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What are props and how do I use them?">
  Props are special power-ups that help you get unstuck during a level. There are three types:

  <CardGroup cols={1}>
    <Card title="Recall One Good" icon="rotate-left">
      Returns one item from your tray back to the shelf. Perfect for undoing an accidental tap or freeing up a slot occupied by an item with no match in sight. **Cost: 100 coins or free via ad.**
    </Card>
    <Card title="Remove 3 Goods" icon="trash">
      Removes 3 goods directly from the shelves (not your tray). Use this to clear items that are physically blocking access to goods you need below them. **Cost: 100 coins or free via ad.**
    </Card>
    <Card title="Rearrange All Goods" icon="shuffle">
      Reshuffles the entire shelf layout, giving you a fresh look at the board. Use when you're completely stuck with no clear matching path. **Cost: 100 coins or free via ad.**
    </Card>
  </CardGroup>

  To use a prop, tap its icon during an active level. If you don't have enough coins, you'll be offered the option to watch an ad to claim it for free instead.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use props for free without spending coins?">
  Yes! Every prop can be claimed for free by watching a short ad instead of spending 100 coins. When you tap a prop during a level, you'll be given the choice to either spend coins or watch an ad.

  <Tip>
    Always consider the free-ad option before spending coins, especially early in a session when your coin balance is still building. Watch the ad, save the coins, and use those coins for a later moment when you need a prop but no ad offer is available.
  </Tip>

  <Note>
    Ad availability can vary. If no ad is currently available, you'll need to spend coins or wait a short time before an ad becomes available again.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

## Account & Technical

<Accordion title="Why did my balance not increase after completing a level?">
  If you've completed a level but don't see your balance go up, here are the most likely explanations:

  - **The reward is below the display threshold** — small increments may accumulate before visibly updating your displayed balance.
  - **The app needs a refresh** — close the app fully and reopen it to trigger a balance sync.
  - **A Daily Mission reward wasn't claimed** — cash tied to missions must be manually claimed from the missions panel before it's added to your balance.
  - **Connection issue** — a brief loss of internet connectivity during the level-complete screen can prevent the reward from registering.

  <Warning>
    If the issue persists after restarting the app and checking your internet connection, reach out to in-app support and include a screenshot of your completed level and current balance for fastest resolution.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>
